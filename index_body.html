The Big Day

When you’re ready to drive, just open the app and trip requests will start coming in. We’ll show you who you’re picking up and provide directions to their location and destination. Once the trip is complete, we’ll find another nearby request. Riders are friendly and respectful of your car. And only you decide when to stop driving for the day.

Let the app lead the way

When you drive with Uber, you decide when and how long to work. So you’ll never have to choose between earning a living and living life.
